I have been interested in making music since 2007, which was the time I started playing drums.
I started composing MIDIs in 2008. Whenever I am handling instrumentation or simply a lot of notes, that's still my way to go.
Since 2012, I have been producing and publishing electronic music under various names.

Tailsnatching (2012 - 2015)

My first project that I published.
Consists mostly of production experiments.
Wanted to combine a progressive approach to composition with atmospheric sounds that feel alive.

A Frame Intact (2014)

The idea was to make music from sounds that were not musical when out of context. Evolved to some deeper experiments and presented other internal atmospheres.

Topal (2015)

Started almost entirely as a sound experiment. Turned into a cold and dead description of which I visualize as a silent native clan that became inhumane but bears an emotional burden at the same time.

Series

Idea Sprout: Summer 2015
First Prototype: Summer 2015, AppGameKit2
Web Prototype: Autumn 2016, HTML5 / JS / Phaser

Rules:

  • 9 Numbers appear on the screen.
  • Press the numbers in the right order.
  • Gain time for each correct press.
  • More numbers appear.
  • Goal: Press numbers in correct order as fast as you can, without timeout.

Colors

Idea Sprout: May 2014
First Prototype: May 2014, Python / PyGame
Web Prototype: April 2017, HTML5 / JS / Phaser

Rules:

  • Cell values: 1-6, Cell Neighbors: Left, Right, Top, Bottom
  • Neighbor has (own value + 1) -> Cell can be incremented short press & release
  • 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6 -> 1
  • Cell cluster with same value can all change at once. long press & release on a changable cell
  • CHANGING CLUSTERS COUNTS FOR A SINGLE MOVE
  • Goal: Turn all cells into the same color in least amount of moves.
